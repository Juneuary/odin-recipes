Depression Meals for Tough Times

Salt Water Soup

Salt Water Soup? Oh boy! We're going all out tonight!!

Times are tough and income inequality is larger than ever. But don't worry, nothing like a salt water soup to take the edge off things

After all, you still need to eat and get your energy back up so you can pull yourself up by the bootstraps
INGREDIENTS
  • 4 cups of water
  • 2 tablespoons of salt

Steps

  1. Add in your cups of water into the a pot
  2. Turn the stove on high
  3. When the water boils, add in your salt and stir till dissolved
  4. When the salt is dissolved, turn off the stove
  5. Pour contents into a bowl and bon appetit!
