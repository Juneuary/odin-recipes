A Depression Meal

Curry Udon noodle Soup: the Ultimate Depression Meal

Description

Feeling too tired or too sad to cook? Understandable,the big SAD is here but you still need to eat. Try this dish where everything is thrown into the pot and simmered and ready under 20 minutes.

INGREDIENTS

  • cabbage: you can shred this with your hands
  • Sanuki Udon noodles
  • Curry Cube
  • chicken broth (any broth will do)
  • thinly sliced beef

Steps

  1. wash the 1/4 head of cabbage
  2. Cut the cabbage into small squares (or tear into small chunks with your hands)
  3. Place a pot on the stove and turn on medium
  4. add 3 cups of chicken broth
  5. add the sanuki udon noodles
  6. add the cut or torn cabbage
  7. add thinly sliced beef (as much as you'd like to eat)
  8. Add the Curry cube and put a lid over the pot
  9. let simmer on medium high for 10 minutes
  10. turn off the stove and mix the contents so the cube is throughly dissolved and mixed throughout the soup
  11. pour into a bowl or enjoy out of the pot
