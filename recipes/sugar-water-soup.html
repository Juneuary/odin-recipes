Sugar Water Soup

Sugar Water Soup? We are getting fancy here tonight, aren't we boys?

You just had salt water soup and now you're craving something a little sweet and have some water lying around? WOOO! Let's go whip something up tonight

Ingredients

  • Sugar(however much you like)
  • 2 cups of water

Steps

  1. Turn the stove onto a medium high
  2. Add in your water and wait for the water to simmer
  3. when the water begins to boil and you can see steam, add your sugar in and stir
  5. when the sugar is dissolved, turn off the stove
  6. Pour the contents into a bowl and enjoy, you silly little rascal
